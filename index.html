It will be open-note, but collaboration is not allowed. We will schedule office hours during the midterm to in order to clarify questions or review material. ### Final Project Either solo or in pairs, students will create agents for an OpenAI environment and demo their agent on the final day of classes. A rubric will be provided when once this project is assigned. ### Assignments We will give a mix of in-class and short homework assignments. The assignments will vary between problem sets and short coding problems.
